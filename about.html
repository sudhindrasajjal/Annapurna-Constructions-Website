About Us

ANNAPURNA Constructions and Interiors, (ACI) is a multi-skilled architectural design company creating a wide variety of branded environments.

Annapurna Constructions & Interiors

We are versed in creating identities that help sell your product. ACI strives to understand its client’s business and support it with credible, attractive, responsive, versatile and lasting solutions. Established as a full service architectural company, specializing in retail design. Our talented team of architects, urban planners, interior and graphic designers provide a broad range of design services.

Our main strength, throughout our decade long service has been the multiplicity and flexibility of our services. We also have a successful and consistent record in stringent quality control apart from our commitments on the precise project time-lines. We offer an expansive range of services packaged in permutations and combinations to suit your specific types, needs and budgets. We know how to adapt our proven management approach to local systems, customs and codes. Most of all how to build alliances and connect with people to create interior/retail and architectural projects of international distinction. While continuing to sustain its reputation in design, ACI enjoys increased recognition in master planning, mixed-use, residential commercial and workspace sectors.

ETHOS

Promote effective design concepts that support client’s business goals and directives. Whether creating a site analysis study that maximizes a developer’s investment, a corporate identity that promotes a business culture, or a retail identity that sells our client’s product, ACI manages projects of varying sizes. Supported by talented architects and designers that create the appropriate technical solutions while maintaining a distinctive and dynamic final product for the clients.

We work as an interface between your business (design) ambitions and the reality; to deliver on time and on budget.

We transliterate the client’s design brief through to the final delivery. We listen and work with you.

ACI combines unique design, international experience with local socio-cultural cognition to provide an effective and comprehensive support for the businesses served.
